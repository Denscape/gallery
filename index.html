About

Black Clover is a fantasy anime set in a world where magic defines everything, yet its main hero, Asta, is born without any magic at all. Together with his talented rival and best friend Yuno, he dreams of becoming the Wizard King, the strongest mage in the Clover Kingdom. After receiving a mysterious five-leaf clover grimoire that grants him anti-magic powers, Asta joins the Black Bulls squad and faces powerful enemies, dark secrets, and intense battles that test his determination and bonds with his friends. The series blends high-energy fights, emotional character growth, and inspiring underdog themes, making it a favorite for many shonen anime fans around the world.
